About My Band

My Band is a group of talented musicians who have been making music together for over 10 years. We play a mix of rock and blues, and our influences include bands like Led Zeppelin and The Rolling Stones.

Band Members

  • John Smith (vocals and guitar)
  • Jane Doe (bass guitar)
  • Bob Johnson (drums)
  • Samantha Williams (keyboards)

Band History

My Band formed in 2010, when John and Jane met at a local music school. They started playing together and quickly realized they had a special chemistry. Bob and Samantha joined the band soon after, and the four of them have been making music together ever since.
